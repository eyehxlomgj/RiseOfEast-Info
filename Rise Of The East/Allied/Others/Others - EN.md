# Allied Powers Overview

## Support Powers

Support Powers provide tactical advantages on the battlefield, ranging from emergency resources to specialized units and abilities. Below is a comprehensive list of available Support Powers.

### Emergency Power

![Emergency Power](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(28).png)

Grants **300 power** for **36 seconds** at no cost. Ideal for powering up critical structures during energy shortages.

- **Provided by**: Allied Conyard
- **Cooldown**: 4 minutes
- **Cost**: Free

### Ambulance Delivery

![Ambulance Delivery](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(30).png)

Deploys an **Ambulance** to your War Factory after a short delay. Essential for healing infantry units.

- **Provided by**: War Factory
- **Cooldown**: 4 minutes
- **Cost**: 600

### Ambulance Unit

![Ambulance Unit](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/AlliedUnits%20(28).jpg)

The **Ambulance** heals multiple infantry units simultaneously, keeping your ground forces combat-ready.

- **Health**: 200
- **Armor**: All Ground Light
- **Speed**: 7
- **Vision Range**: 7
- **Weapon Range**: 1.83

### Sonar Scan

![Sonar Scan](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(31).png)

Temporarily reveals **submerged navy** and **stealth units**, countering hidden threats.

- **Provided by**: Radar Tower
- **Cooldown**: 3 minutes
- **Cost**: Free

### Airstrike

![Airstrike](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(7).png)

Calls in an **A10 airstrike** with increasing intensity per tier (1/2/3 bombers). Deals significant damage in the targeted area.

- **Provided by**: Radar Tower
- **Cooldown**: 5 minutes
- **Cost**: 500/750/1000 (based on tier)

### Gap Generator

![Gap Generator](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(32).png)

Creates a **shroud** anywhere on the battlefield, obscuring enemy vision. The generator is destroyed after a short duration.

- **Provided by**: Battle Lab
- **Cooldown**: 4 minutes
- **Cost**: 500

### Cryo HE

![Cryo HE](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(17).png)

Enhances **Tanya's** abilities, allowing her slow effects to affect an area, stack, and fire **50% faster**. Reduces her damage by **50%**.

- **Provided by**: Tanya
- **Cooldown**: 2 minutes
- **Cost**: Free

### Tether

![Tether](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(33).png)

Teleports **Jaeger** anywhere on the battlefield for strategic positioning or harassment.

- **Provided by**: Jaeger
- **Cooldown**: 4 minutes
- **Cost**: Free

### Angel Beacon

![Angel Beacon](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(10).png)

Places an **Angel Beacon** that allows **Yuki** to attack within **35 tiles**, dealing massive area damage.

- **Provided by**: Yuki
- **Cooldown**: 3 minutes
- **Cost**: Free

## Upgrades

Upgrades enhance your units' capabilities, providing permanent boosts to specific factions or units. Below are the available upgrades.

### TOW Missiles

![TOW Missiles](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(29).png)

Equips **Humvees** with missiles, increasing damage against armored ground targets.

- **Provided by**: War Factory
- **Available to**: All Allies
- **Cost**: 850

### Air Superiority

![Air Superiority](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(9).png)

Grants **free veterancy** to newly created jets and enables **F22 Raptors** to be built as **King Raptors** with an intercept laser.

- **Provided by**: Airfield
- **Available to**: America
- **Cost**: 1750

### Cryo Guns

![Cryo Guns](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(15).png)

Boosts **Tengu** damage and armor by **20%**, and **Tsunami** damage by **20%** and armor by **13%**.

- **Provided by**: Battle Lab
- **Available to**: Japan
- **Cost**: 2000

## General Powers

General Powers are faction-specific abilities that unlock powerful units, buffs, or support powers. Organized by faction: **America**, **Germany**, and **Japan**.

### America

#### Paradrop

![Paradrop](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(34).png)

Deploys a mix of **infantry units** anywhere on the battlefield, scaling with tier.

- **Tier**: 1
- **Available to**: America
- **Provided by**: Conyard
- **Cooldown**: 6 minutes

#### Search and Destroy

![Search and Destroy](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(3).png)

Unlocks the **Crusader Tank**, which deals **10% more damage** against T1 units compared to the Grizzly Tank. Grants **15% speed** to all units permanently.

- **Tier**: 1
- **Available to**: America
- **Provided by**: Conyard

#### Readiness

![Readiness](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(1).png)

Equips infantry with a **Kevlar Vest** (10/20/30 damage shield based on tier) and increases veterancy gain speed by **15%**.

- **Tier**: 2
- **Available to**: America
- **Provided by**: Arsenal

#### Drone Carrier

![Drone Carrier](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(20).png)

Enables construction of the **Drone Carrier**, replacing the Shatterer.

- **Tier**: 2
- **Available to**: America
- **Provided by**: Arsenal

#### Paladin Tank

![Paladin Tank](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(27).png)

Unlocks the **Paladin Tank**, replacing the M1A1 Abrams. Equipped with an interceptor laser to shoot down missiles.

- **Tier**: 3
- **Available to**: America
- **Provided by**: Battle Lab

#### M.O.A.B.

![M.O.A.B.](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(26).png)

Grants access to the **M.O.A.B.** support power, calling a bomber that deals massive area damage.

- **Tier**: 3
- **Available to**: America
- **Provided by**: Battle Lab
- **Cooldown**: 8 minutes

### Germany

#### E.M.S. Missile

![E.M.S. Missile](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(4).png)

Deploys a bomber that **slows**, **disables weapons**, and reduces **veterancy** of enemy units by one stage.

- **Tier**: 1
- **Available to**: Germany
- **Provided by**: Conyard
- **Cooldown**: 5.5 minutes

#### Laser Comanche

![Laser Comanche](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(23).png)

Replaces the standard Comanche with the **Laser Comanche**, dealing **20% extra base damage**.

- **Tier**: 1
- **Available to**: Germany
- **Provided by**: Conyard

#### Shard Burst

![Shard Burst](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(35).png)

Afflicts enemy vehicles with a status that may **explode** for damage every **10 seconds** (20/40/60 seconds based on tier).

- **Tier**: 2
- **Available to**: Germany
- **Provided by**: Arsenal
- **Cooldown**: 5 minutes

#### Refractor

![Refractor](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(2).png)

Enables construction of **Refractors**, support units that buff nearby prism/laser units with a stackable buff.

- **Tier**: 2
- **Available to**: Germany
- **Provided by**: Arsenal

#### Nova Tank

![Nova Tank](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(37).png)

Unlocks the **Nova Tank**, highly effective against vehicles.

- **Tier**: 3
- **Available to**: Germany
- **Provided by**: Battle Lab

#### Phase Shield

![Phase Shield](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(36).png)

Applies a **50 HP shield** to a target, refreshing every minute. Boosts **Laser Tank** and **Prism Defender** HP by **15%**.

- **Tier**: 3
- **Available to**: Germany
- **Provided by**: Battle Lab
- **Cooldown**: 6 minutes

### Japan

#### Imperial Munitions

![Imperial Munitions](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(22).png)

Increases **Jieitai** and **Tengu** damage by **20%**. Unlocks the **Beam Katana** support power, enhancing Jieitai with melee capabilities, movement speed, and **10% HP**.

- **Tier**: 1
- **Available to**: Japan
- **Provided by**: Conyard
- **Cooldown**: 4 minutes

#### Tempest Pike

![Tempest Pike](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(6).png)

Spawns a temporary **Tempest Spike** building that attacks multiple enemies (2/3/4 based on tier).

- **Tier**: 1
- **Available to**: Japan
- **Provided by**: Conyard
- **Cooldown**: 4 minutes

#### Maiden Remnants

![Maiden Remnants](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(25).png)

Spawns a temporary **remnant** at an **Archer Maiden's** death location, dealing damage to nearby enemies.

- **Tier**: 2
- **Available to**: Japan
- **Provided by**: Arsenal

#### Buster Surprise

![Buster Surprise](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(12).png)

Spawns a group of **Cryobusters** (1/3/5 based on tier) anywhere on the battlefield.

- **Tier**: 2
- **Available to**: Japan
- **Provided by**: Arsenal
- **Cooldown**: 5.5/5/4.5 minutes

#### Ice Rink

![Ice Rink](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(21).png)

Temporarily **freezes** vehicles or buildings, preventing movement, damage dealt, or received.

- **Tier**: 3
- **Available to**: Japan
- **Provided by**: Battle Lab
- **Cooldown**: 6 minutes

#### Cryo Strike

![Cryo Strike](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Alliedspecialcameo%20(16).png)

Calls a bomber that deals damage, **slows**, and potentially **freezes** units based on hit frequency.

- **Tier**: 3
- **Available to**: Japan
- **Provided by**: Battle Lab
- **Cooldown**: 6 minutes
