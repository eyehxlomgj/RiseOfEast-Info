# Allied Faction Buildings

## Allied Conyard

![Allied Conyard](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/AlliedBuildings%20(3).jpg)

The Construction Yard is the cornerstone of any base, enabling commanders to construct buildings and allowing Allied commanders to build in its vicinity.

- **Cost**: 2500
- **Power**: +50

---

## Power Plant

![Power Plant](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/AlliedBuildings%20(9).jpg)

The Power Plant is the primary power source for Allied factions. Monitor power levels in-game by checking the top-right corner or hovering over the power bar next to the command card.

- **Cost**: 800
- **Power**: +200

---

## Barracks

![Barracks](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/AlliedBuildings%20(4).jpg)

Required for training infantry, additional Barracks increase unit build speed, though they do not provide extra queues. The number needed for maximum build speed depends on the unit's cost.

- **Cost**: 500
- **Power**: 0

---

## Ore Refinery

![Ore Refinery](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/AlliedBuildings%20(5).jpg)

A critical economic building, the Ore Refinery processes ore from miners to generate income. Protect it with guard dogs to counter enemy spies.

- **Cost**: 2300
- **Power**: -50

---

## War Factory

![War Factory](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/AlliedBuildings%20(11).jpg)

Essential for vehicle production, the War Factory also unlocks the Ambulance support power and TOW Missile upgrade. Spying on it grants your vehicles a veterancy rank upon creation.

- **Cost**: 2000
- **Power**: -50

---

## Airfield

![Airfield](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/AlliedBuildings%20(6).jpg)

The Airfield enables jet production and includes a helipad for choppers using the jet queue. Set waypoints for choppers post-construction via the interface.

- **Cost**: 1000
- **Power**: -50

---

## Runway

![Runway](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/AlliedBuildings%20(22).jpg)

Expands aircraft capacity, providing space for four additional jets.

- **Cost**: 1000
- **Power**: -25

---

## Shipyard

![Shipyard](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/AlliedBuildings%20(8).jpg)

Available on water-heavy maps, the Shipyard grants access to naval units.

- **Cost**: 1500
- **Power**: -40

---

## Radar Tower

![Radar Tower](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/AlliedBuildings%20(7).jpg)

Provides minimap access, infantry production, and the expansion vehicle. Spying resets scouting. Unlocks Sonar Scan and, for America, Airstrike.

- **Cost**: 1250
- **Power**: -100

---

## Arsenal

![Arsenal](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/AlliedBuildings%20(13).jpg)

Unlocks Tier 2 units and General Powers. Place it strategically to protect access to advanced units.

- **Cost**: 750
- **Power**: -150

---

## Ore Purifier

![Ore Purifier](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/AlliedBuildings%20(16).jpg)

Boosts economy by enhancing ore value and power plant output. Unlocks Processor and refinement buildings for secondary economy development.

- **Cost**: 2500
- **Power**: -250

---

## Processor

![Processor](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/AlliedBuildings%20(17).jpg)

Functions like an Ore Refinery but without a miner, making it a cost-effective option for expanding your secondary economy.

- **Cost**: 1100
- **Power**: -40

---

## Battle Lab

![Battle Lab](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/AlliedBuildings%20(18).jpg)

Grants access to Tier 3 units, structures, and General Powers. Spying on it allows enemies to access the Juggernaut. Keep it secure.

- **Cost**: 3500
- **Power**: -300

---

## Spy Satellite

![Spy Satellite](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/AlliedBuildings%20(20).jpg)

Reveals the entire map, aiding scouting efforts.

- **Cost**: 1500
- **Power**: -200

---

## Chronosphere

![Chronosphere](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/AlliedBuildings%20(19).jpg)

The Allied minor superweapon, enabling teleportation of friendly or enemy units to any location.

- **Cost**: 3000
- **Power**: -250

---

## Satellite Uplink

![Satellite Uplink](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/AlliedBuildings%20(2).jpg)

Deploys a powerful, mobile beam to deal significant damage to enemy armies. Effective against moving targets when given move commands.

- **Cost**: 6000
- **Power**: -350

---

## Weather Controller

![Weather Controller](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/AlliedBuildings%20(23).jpg)

Creates lightning clouds over a targeted area to deal massive area damage.

- **Cost**: 6000
- **Power**: -350

---

## Concrete Wall

![Concrete Wall](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/AlliedBuildings%20(21).jpg)

Blocks enemy spies and engineers. Chain walls within a certain range for efficient placement.

- **Cost**: 75
- **Power**: 0

---

## Pillbox

![Pillbox](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/AlliedBuildings%20(10).jpg)

An early-game defense effective against rush strategies, dealing solid damage to early units.

- **Cost**: 600
- **Power**: 0
- **Weapon Range**: 6

---

## Gun Turret

![Gun Turret](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/AlliedBuildings%20(14).jpg)

Water-based defense to protect Shipyards.

- **Cost**: 1000
- **Power**: -30
- **Weapon Range**: 8

---

## Missile Site

![Missile Site](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/AlliedBuildings%20(12).jpg)

Anti-air defense effective against air units, though multiple sites may be needed for stronger threats like Kirovs.

- **Cost**: 800
- **Power**: -30
- **Weapon Range**: 14

---
