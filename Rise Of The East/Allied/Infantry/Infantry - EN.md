# Allied Faction Infantry Units

## Attack Dog

![Attack Dog](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/AlliedInfantry%20(4).jpg)

The Attack Dog is a reliable companion for scouting and detecting enemy spies. It excels at countering disguised units and engaging enemy infantry.

- **Cost**: 180
- **HP**: 112
- **Armor**: Dogarm
- **Speed**: 9
- **Vision Range**: 9
- **Weapon Range**: 1.83
- **Prerequisite**: Barracks
- **Role**: Scouting, Anti-Infantry, Anti-Disguise
