# Allied Faction Units

## Chrono Miner

![Chrono Miner](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/AlliedUnits%20(2).jpg)

The Chrono Miner is the primary income source for the Allied army, using its chronowarp ability to escape threats and return to refineries quickly.

- **Cost**: 1200
- **HP**: 700
- **Armor**: T2medium
- **Speed**: 6
- **Vision Range**: 4
- **Weapon Range**: None
- **Prerequisite**: Refinery
- **Role**: Economy

## Humvee

![Humvee](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/AlliedUnits%20(26).jpg)

A fast unit that deals significant damage when used effectively. It can carry 2 Rangers, GIs, or Jieitai, allowing them to fire from inside. Upgradable with the TOW Missile for enhanced anti-vehicle capability.

- **Cost**: 500
- **HP**: 200
- **Armor**: Light
- **Speed**: 7
- **Vision Range**: 7
