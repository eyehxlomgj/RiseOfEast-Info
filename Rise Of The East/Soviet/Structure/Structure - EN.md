# Soviet Structures

## Core Structures

### Soviet Conyard

![Soviet Conyard](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Buildings%20(1).png)

The foundation for constructing structures. Multiple Conyards increase building speed.

- **Cost**: 3000
- **Power**: +50

### Tesla Reactor

![Tesla Reactor](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Buildings%20(4).png)

Provides essential **power** for Soviet bases until the Nuclear Reactor is available.

- **Cost**: 800
- **Power**: +200

### Soviet Barracks

![Soviet Barracks](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Buildings%20(6).png)

Required for **infantry production**. Additional barracks increase production speed. Grants **infantry veterancy** upon spy infiltration.

- **Cost**: 750
- **Power**: 0

### Soviet Refinery

![Soviet Refinery](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Buildings%20(13).png)

Converts ore from Warminers into **income**. Multiple refineries ensure economic stability. Spy infiltration steals funds if available.

- **Cost**: 2300
- **Power**: +50

### Soviet Warfactory

![Soviet Warfactory](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Buildings%20(2).png)

Produces **vehicles**. More factories increase production speed. Grants **vehicle veterancy** upon spy infiltration.

- **Cost**: 2000
- **Power**: +50

### Soviet Airfield

![Soviet Airfield](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Buildings%20(17).png)

Required for **jet production**. Grants **jet veterancy** upon spy infiltration.

- **Cost**: 1000
- **Power**: -30

### Soviet Runway

![Soviet Runway](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Buildings%20(18).png)

Expands **jet capacity** for additional air units.

- **Cost**: 1000
- **Power**: -25

### Soviet Shipyard

![Soviet Shipyard](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Buildings%20(16).png)

Builds **ships**. Defend with nearby structures to protect against naval attacks.

- **Cost**: 1500
- **Power**: -40

### Radar Tower

![Radar Tower](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Buildings%20(5).png)

Unlocks the **minimap** and key **support powers**. Spy infiltration resets scouting.

- **Cost**: 1250
- **Power**: -100

### Soviet Armory

![Soviet Armory](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Buildings%20(3).png)

Grants access to **Tier 2 support powers, General Powers (GPs), and units**. A critical tech structure.

- **Cost**: 1750
- **Power**: -150

### Industrial Plant

![Industrial Plant](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Buildings%20(14).png)

Reduces **vehicle costs** by 10% and enables **secondary economy**. For Russia, unlocks Unit Reprocessor and Industrial Factory for Scrap Tanks. Other factions spawn scrap near factories.

- **Cost**: 2500
- **Power**: -350

### Industrial Factory

![Industrial Factory](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Buildings%20(19).png)

Produces a **Scrap Tank** for each vehicle built, vital for secondary economy.

- **Cost**: 2000
- **Power**: -100

### Unit Reprocessor

![Unit Reprocessor](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Buildings%20(20).png)

Recycles **vehicles** for resources, occasionally producing **Scrap Tanks**. Immediate recycling of Scrap Tanks maximizes funds.

- **Cost**: 2500
- **Power**: -150

### Tactical Nuke Launcher

![Tactical Nuke Launcher](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Buildings%20(21).png)

Exclusive to Iraq with the **Tactical Nuke GP**. Spreads **radiation** when hitting enemy structures.

- **Cost**: 1500
- **Power**: -150

### Battle Lab

![Battle Lab](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Buildings%20(7).png)

Unlocks **Tier 3 units, buildings, support powers, and GPs**. Spy infiltration grants the enemy **Mammoth Tank** access.

- **Cost**: 3500
- **Power**: -300

### Mechabay

![Mechabay](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Buildings%20(23).png)

Exclusive to Ukraine with the **Mechabay GP**. Supports construction of the **Goliath** unit.

- **Cost**: 2000
- **Power**: -250

### Nuclear Reactor

![Nuclear Reactor](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Buildings%20(10).png)

Provides **massive power** but causes a large explosion if destroyed. Place in a safe location.

- **Cost**: 2000
- **Power**: +2000

### Iron Curtain

![Iron Curtain](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Buildings%20(9).png)

A **minor superweapon** granting vehicles a temporary **invulnerability shield**. Upgrades to **Iron Nova** for a reduced effect upon damage.

- **Cost**: 3000
- **Power**: -200

### Nuke Silo

![Nuke Silo](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Buildings%20(11).png)

The **major superweapon**, launching a nuke for massive damage and leaving a **radiated area**.

- **Cost**: 5000
- **Power**: -200

## Defensive Structures

### Concrete Walls

![Concrete Walls](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Buildings(26).png)

Prevents **spies** and **engineers** from accessing buildings. Connect for efficient placement.

- **Cost**: 75
- **Power**: 0

### Sentry Gun

![Sentry Gun](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Buildings(24).png)

A **low-tier defense** effective against early enemy rushes. Support with nearby units.

- **Cost**: 600
- **Power**: 0

### Battle Bunker

![Battle Bunker](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Buildings%20(8).png)

A **strong defense** that can be garrisoned by infantry for increased firepower.

- **Cost**: 500
- **Power**: 0

### Flak Cannon

![Flak Cannon](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Buildings%20(12).png)

Counters **enemy air units**. Build multiple to protect key structures.

- **Cost**: 800
- **Power**: -30

### Tesla Coil

![Tesla Coil](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Buildings%20(25).png)

A **Tier 2 defense** effective against all ground units. Overcharge with a **Tesla Trooper** for increased damage and passive veterancy.

- **Cost**: 1200
- **Power**: -50

### Naval Missile Battery

![Naval Missile Battery](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Buildings%20(15).png)

A **water-based defense** to protect shipyards from enemy naval units.

- **Cost**: 1200
- **Power**: -40

### Iron Fist

![Iron Fist](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Buildings(22).png)

A **defensive support structure** applying a temporary **Iron Curtain effect** to nearby units, though enemies can reduce its duration with damage.

- **Cost**: 1500
- **Power**: -250
