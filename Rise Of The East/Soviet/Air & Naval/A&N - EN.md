# Soviet Air & Naval Units
## Air Units

### Sukhoi

![Sukhoi](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/sukhoi_03.png)

A versatile jet capable of engaging both **ground** and **air targets**, making it a cornerstone of Soviet air forces.

- **Cost**: 1000
- **HP**: 180
- **Armor**: T1 Air
- **Speed**: 17
- **Vision Range**: 8
- **Weapon Range**: 10
- **Prerequisite**: Airfield
- **Role**: Anti-Vehicle, Anti-Air

### SU30 Tesla

![SU30 Tesla](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Teslajet_03.png)

Equipped with **EMP missiles**, the SU30 Tesla delivers heavy **AoE damage** to ground targets and excels against enemy air units.

- **Cost**: 1300
- **HP**: 225
- **Armor**: T1 Air
- **Speed**: 17
- **Vision Range**: 8
- **Weapon Range**: 10
- **Prerequisite**: Armory, Blackout GP
- **Role**: Anti-Vehicle, Anti-Air

## Naval Units

### Transport Hovercraft

![Transport Hovercraft](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/sovnavy_09.png)

Essential for **amphibious transport**, the hovercraft moves infantry and vehicles to remote islands for base-building or capturing neutral structures.

- **Cost**: 900
- **HP**: 420
- **Armor**: T2 Heavy
- **Speed**: 7
- **Vision Range**: 6
- **Weapon Range**: None
- **Prerequisite**: Shipyard
- **Role**: Amphibious Transport

### Sea Scorpion

![Sea Scorpion](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/sovnavy_14.png)

A fast boat equipped with a **flak cannon**, ideal for intercepting enemy air units due to its high speed.

- **Cost**: 750
- **HP**: 560
- **Armor**: Heavy
- **Speed**: 9
- **Vision Range**: 8
- **Weapon Range**: 6 (Ground) | 15 (Air)
- **Prerequisite**: Shipyard
- **Role**: Anti-Air

### Typhoon Attack Sub

![Typhoon Attack Sub](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/sovnavy_19.png)

A **submerged** unit specializing in ambushing heavily armored enemy naval units.

- **Cost**: 850
- **HP**: 700
- **Armor**: T1 Ship
- **Speed**: 7
- **Vision Range**: 4
- **Weapon Range**: 10
- **Prerequisite**: Shipyard
