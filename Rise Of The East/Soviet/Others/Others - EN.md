# Soviet Support Powers, Upgrades, and General Powers

## Support Powers

### Sonar Scan

![Sonar Scan](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/yurispecialcameo%20(22).png)

Temporarily reveals **stealth units**, exposing hidden threats.

- **Provided by**: Soviet Radar Tower
- **Cooldown**: 4 minutes
- **Cost**: Free

### Short Circuit

![Short Circuit](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/ttnkv1icon.png)

Grants access to the **Anti-Infantry Tesla Tank** (exclusive choice with Coil Guns).

- **Cost**: Free
- **Cooldown**: None
- **Stats**: See Vehicles section

### Coil Guns

![Coil Guns](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/ttnkv2icon.png)

Grants access to the **Anti-Vehicle Tesla Tank** (exclusive choice with Short Circuit).

- **Cost**: Free
- **Cooldown**: None
- **Stats**: See Vehicles section

### Spy Plane

![Spy Plane](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/SovSpecialCameo%20(26).png)

Reveals the **targeted area** with a spy plane for enhanced scouting.

- **Provided by**: Airfield
- **Cooldown**: 3 minutes
- **Cost**: Free

### Call for Transport

![Call for Transport](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/SovSpecialCameo%20(8).png)

Summons a **transport chopper** at the airfield for moving units.

- **Provided by**: Airfield
- **Cooldown**: 2.5 minutes
- **Cost**: 800

### MI-24

![MI-24](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Untitled-8_20.png)

A **transport chopper** from Call for Transport, capable of carrying both **vehicles** and **infantry**.

- **HP**: 300
- **Armor**: T2 Air
- **Speed**: 35
- **Passengers**: 4

### Chitzkoi

![Chitzkoi](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/SovSpecialCameo%20(5).png)

Summons **Boris’s cyborg companion**, capable of **EMPing** enemy vehicles by biting their wheels.

- **Provided by**: Boris
- **Cooldown**: 4 minutes
- **Cost**: Free

### Tesla Sentry

![Tesla Sentry](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/SovSpecialCameo%20(25).png)

Spawns a **small Tesla Sentry** near Ahsan for defensive support.

- **Provided by**: Ahsan
- **Cooldown**: 2 minutes
- **Cost**: Free

### Vlad’s Bike

![Vlad’s Bike](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/SovSpecialCameo%20(1).png)

Allows **Vladislav** to summon a **bike** for enhanced mobility anywhere on the battlefield.

- **Provided by**: Vladislav
- **Cooldown**: 4 minutes
- **Cost**: Free

## General Powers

### Russia

#### Iron Shell

![Iron Shell](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/SovSpecialCameo%20(11).png)

Increases **Terror Drone** suppression resistance by **50%** and grants a **1-hit blocker** at Tier 2.

- **Tier**: 1
- **Available to**: Russia
- **Provided by**: Conyard
- **Cooldown**: None

#### Short Circuit

![Short Circuit](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/SovSpecialCameo%20(21).png)

Releases a **global shockwave** with a **25% chance** to disable enemy vehicles for **20 seconds**.

- **Tier**: 1
- **Available to**: Russia
- **Provided by**: Conyard
- **Cooldown**: 5 minutes

#### Blackout

![Blackout](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/SovSpecialCameo%20(3).png)

Grants access to the **SU-30T fighter jet** and **Tesla Raider**, both equipped with **EMP weaponry** (replaces Shock Trooper).

- **Tier**: 2
- **Available to**: Russia
- **Provided by**: Arsenal

#### Rocket Retrofitting

![Rocket Retrofitting](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/SovSpecialCameo%20(20).png)

Equips **Spetsnaz** with **hoverpacks** and special weaponry, transforming them into **Conductors**.

- **Tier**: 2
- **Available to**: Russia
- **Provided by**: Armory
- **Cooldown**: 5 minutes

#### Conductor

![Conductor](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/sovinfs%20(5).png)

Upgraded **Spetsnaz** with hoverpacks and specialized weaponry for **anti-air** and **anti-infantry** roles.

- **Tier**: 2
- **Role**: Anti-Air, Anti-Infantry

#### Electric Surge

![Electric Surge](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/SovSpecialCameo%20(23).png)

Permanently **overcharges Tesla Coils** and equips **TTS-1 MLRS** with special ammo that leaves **residual flames** and **Tesla bounces**.

- **Tier**: 3
- **Available to**: Russia
- **Provided by**: Battle Lab

#### Overcharge

![Overcharge](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/SovSpecialCameo%20(16).png)

Grants **Tesla units** a **15% rate-of-fire buff** and access to the **Overcharge support power**, providing a **1-hit blocker** that buffs nearby units’ rate-of-fire upon destruction.

- **Tier**: 3
- **Available to**: Russia
- **Provided by**: Battle Lab
- **Cooldown**: 5 minutes

### Iraq

#### Mercenary

![Mercenary](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/SovSpecialCameo%20(14).png)

Replaces **Conscript** with **Mercenary** and grants the **Combat Armor support power**, providing a tiered shield to infantry in a target area.

- **Tier**: 1
- **Available to**: Iraq
- **Provided by**: Conyard
- **Cooldown**: 6 minutes

#### Tank Drop

![Tank Drop](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/SovSpecialCameo%20(24).png)

Drops **tanks** anywhere on the battlefield, with the number increasing by tier.

- **Tier**: 1
- **Available to**: Iraq
- **Provided by**: Conyard
- **Cooldown**: 6 minutes

#### Field Bunker

![Field Bunker](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/SovSpecialCameo%20(7).png)

Builds a **Field Bunker** anywhere on the map (if space allows), garrisoned with units that scale in strength by tier.

- **Tier**: 2
- **Available to**: Iraq
- **Provided by**: Armory
- **Cooldown**: 5 minutes

#### Radiation Missile

![Radiation Missile](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/SovSpecialCameo%20(18).png)

Grants access to the **Radiation Missile Silo**, allowing periodic launches of **radiation missiles** that spread more radiation on structures.

- **Tier**: 2
- **Available to**: Iraq
- **Provided by**: Armory
- **Cooldown**: 6 minutes

#### Indoctrination

![Indoctrination](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/SovSpecialCameo%20(9).png)

Equips **Kirov** with a **speaker tower**, increasing nearby ground units’ speed by **30%** and Kirov’s speed by **2**.

- **Tier**: 3
- **Available to**: Iraq
- **Provided by**: Battle Lab

#### Nuclear Warheads

![Nuclear Warheads](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/SovSpecialCameo%20(15).png)

Upgrades **Scorpion Tank** with **nuclear weapons** and **+1 speed**, and equips **Demolishers** with **nuclear mortars** via the Armament support power.

- **Tier**: 3
- **Available to**: Iraq
- **Provided by**: Battle Lab
- **Cooldown**: 6 minutes

### Ukraine

#### Bulava Upgrade

![Bulava Upgrade](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/SovSpecialCameo%20(12).png)

Upgrades the **Bulava Tank** to be **amphibious**, with **24% increased HP** and **+1 speed**.

- **Tier**: 1
- **Available to**: Ukraine
- **Provided by**: Conyard

#### Strider Flank

![Strider Flank](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/SovSpecialCameo%20(22).png)

Spawns a group of **Striders** (2/3/4 by tier) anywhere on the battlefield, with a **Sickle** at Tier 3.

- **Tier**: 1
- **Available to**: Ukraine
- **Provided by**: Conyard
- **Cooldown**: 6 minutes

#### Ammo Reserves

![Ammo Reserves](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/SovSpecialCameo%20(2).png)

Increases **aircraft reload speed** by **30%**, **ground unit rate-of-fire** by **10%**, and adds **1 ammo** to the **Hammerhead**.

- **Tier**: 2
- **Available to**: Ukraine
- **Provided by**: Armory

#### Reactive Armor

![Reactive Armor](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/SovSpecialCameo%20(19).png)

Adds **15% HP** to **light and medium armored vehicles**.

- **Tier**: 2
- **Available to**: Ukraine
- **Provided by**: Armory

#### Iron Nova

![Iron Nova](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/SovSpecialCameo%20(10).png)

Replaces **Iron Curtain**, providing vehicles a **1-hit shield** with a reduced Iron Curtain effect upon destruction. Grants access to **Iron Guard infantry**.

- **Tier**: 3
- **Available to**: Ukraine
- **Provided by**: Battle Lab

#### Mechabay

![Mechabay](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/SovSpecialCameo%20(13).png)

Unlocks the **Mechabay**, required to build the **Goliath**.

- **Tier**: 3
- **Available to**: Ukraine
- **Provided by**: Battle Lab
