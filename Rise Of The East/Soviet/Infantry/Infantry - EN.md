# Soviet Infantry Units

### Soviet Attack Dog

![Soviet Attack Dog](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/Untitled-23_03.png)

A fast and agile unit ideal for **scouting** and eliminating enemy infantry and spies. Station near key structures to counter infiltrators.

- **Cost**: 180
- **HP**: 112
- **Armor**: Dog Armor
- **Speed**: 9
- **Vision Range**: 9
- **Weapon Range**: 1.83
- **Prerequisite**: Barracks
- **Role**: Scouting, Anti-Infantry, Anti-Disguise

### Soviet War Bear

![Soviet War Bear](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/sovinfs%20(11).png)

- **Cost**: 300

### Soviet Spy

![Soviet Spy](https://www.riseoftheeastmod.com/assets/img/sovinfs%20(4).png)

Disguises as enemy infantry to infiltrate buildings, granting **veterancy** (Barracks, War Factory, Airfield), **stealing funds** (Refinery), resetting **minimap** (Radar Tower), or unlocking **stolen tech** (Battle Lab).

- **Cost**: 800
- **HP**: 140
- **Armor**: Spy Armor
- **Speed**: 6
- **Vision Range**: 9

- **Prerequisite**: Armory
